How to Play

Objective:

Resistance wins if 3 Missions succeed. Spies win if 3 Missions fail or 5 team votes are rejected in one round.

Phases:

  • 1. Team Building: The Leader picks a team of a specific size.
  • 2. Voting: Everyone votes Approve or Reject. A tied vote is a rejection.
  • 3. Mission: Team members secretly choose Success or Fail (Resistance MUST choose Success).

Special Rule:

In games of 7+ players, the 4th mission requires TWO Fails cards to sabotage the mission.
